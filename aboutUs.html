About us

Shehnayi is a match-making company exclusively for sindhi community. We are trying to resolve the problem and shorten the long-time taking process of finding someone special for you. Get a perfect match by using modern technology but still following the traditional values and cultures. We are here to serve you in a best possible way.

Mission

Our mission and objectives are to serve our community by providing genuine and verified profile information, to shorten the long-time taking process of searching matches and help them to find their perfect life partner. We are trying to do this with the help of advance technology to make the process easier and more convenient for people to search and select the suitable matches for them.
